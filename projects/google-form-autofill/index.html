Google Form Autofill

Google Workspace JavaScript Google Apps Script Automation

This project uses Google Apps Script to automate the National Chinese Honor Society’s member application process by collecting responses from a Google Form, populating an official application PDF, and emailing the completed document to the applicant. What began as a small Apps Script experiment evolved into a full automation workflow, emphasizing scripting logic, document generation, and integration across Google Workspace tools. While originally built for my school's chapter of NCHS, the project’s focus is on scalable automation rather than manual administrative work.
